From the UK, with love
Despite the British shores being a thortful card’s birthplace, that doesn’t stop them from getting their little passport, packing their little bag, and hopping off on an overseas adventure.

From sunny Australia to snowy Canada, thortful employees and customers alike have been sharing the love far and wide this holiday season.

Now that Christmas is over, we wanted to see where these cards ended up but more importantly how they got there are why.
Australia
"I moved to the UK 10 years ago and my Mum and Dad still live back in Australia . Sending them these cards, with a bit of humour, helps me show I care and keep us connected as a family. I would always go for something cheeky/funny/rude because that's my family's humour"
- Luke
"This card was sent to my brother and his partner in sunny Brisbane where they are experiencing 35 degree heat. I sent it to them not only to wish them a Merry Christmas but to remind them of the very snowy Christmas that they spent with us last year. This card captured the contrast perfectly!"
- Sam
Europe
"I got this for my Granny in Spain ! When I was younger she used to come over for Christmas and we used to go ice skating together. The cat is also a spitting image of her own so this card felt like the perfect match and she loved it so much!"
- Maria
"My best friend Sophie has been living in France with her partner. She usually comes back for the holidays but isn't this year. I miss her but I’m happy for her."
- Ben
"My friend Thayna is in Portuga l so I wanted to send her some festive wishes. I also love this card and thought she would too."
- Lee
"My best friend took a massive leap to go and live in Vienna for a fantastic job opportunity and I miss her everyday. She's an unidentified exhibitionist and the strongest girl boss I know so the Christmas card was perfect."
- Louise
"I know my friend Dimitra in Greece has a very busy December with work so I sent her a card to lift her spirits and hype her up ahead of the festive season."
- Alfie
North America
"My husband and I left all of our family and friends back in Canada . We don’t visit in the winter (it’s way too cold) but we want to make sure they know we’re thinking of them around the holidays. Plus my friend Maddison is a massive cat mum so the card was purrfect."
- Aleesha
"My best friend and I share a passion for two things: Christmas and the Spice Girls, so I knew I needed to get her this card! She lives in LA in America and I only see her once or twice a year, but we send each other lots of cards because friendship never eeeeends"
- Laura
